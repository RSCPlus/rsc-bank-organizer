RSC Bank Organizer

Version 2021.01

Features

  • Drag and drop items to sort items
  • Hover over items to see the item name
  • Right click items to turn the background red (good for tracking what you have)
  • Import order from RSC+
  • Export order to RSC+
  • Add and remove items from grid
